Solvents aren't just a compliance issue. They're a people issue.

The cost of hazardous cleaning goes far beyond the bill for the chemicals. It shows up in your H&S liability, your machine downtime, your waste disposal contracts — and in the health of your workforce.

Who We Work With

Your industry. Your problem. Our solution.

blent is designed for manufacturers who use hazardous or ineffective cleaning products as part of their process.

01
Ink Manufacturing
Solvent-based cleaning between ink runs is one of the largest sources of VOC exposure on manufacturing floors. Cross-contamination risk, slow changeovers, and the cost of hazardous waste disposal are endemic to the industry.
Ink & Pigment
02
Lithographic Printing
Press cleaning with isopropyl alcohol and hydrocarbon solvents is standard — and largely unquestioned. The VOC exposure on print floors is significant, and the regulatory pressure is building.
Print & Litho
03
Resin Manufacturing
Vessel and equipment cleaning between batches relies heavily on acetone, MEK, and other aggressive solvents. ATEX zoning, long clean cycles, and the chemistry involved represent one of the highest H&S risk profiles in manufacturing.
Resin & Polymer
04
Paint & Coatings
Mixing vessels, spray equipment, and coated substrates all require regular cleaning. Cured coatings and conventional solvents create some of the most stubborn cleaning problems in manufacturing — and the most hazardous.
Paint & Coatings
